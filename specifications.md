
**Color Pallete**

**Light Mode**
- Primary (60%): Sandstone/Bone White (e.g., #F4EFEB). A clean, warm neutral for backgrounds that makes your menu photography and food pop. 
- Secondary (30%): Terracotta / Multani Clay (e.g., #C85A32). An earthy, inviting shade of deep rusty-orange for footers, large headings, and block quotes. 
- Accent (10%): Mughal Teal / Firozi (e.g., #008080) or Antique Gold (e.g., #D4AF37). Use sparingly for Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, navigation links, or to border the reservation form. 
- Text/Dark: Charcoal / Dark Umber (e.g., #2C2621). Avoid solid black for text to maintain a cozy, welcoming feel.

**Dark Mode**
- Primary (60%): Deep Charcoal Umber (e.g., #1C1816). A rich, dark brown-black for main backgrounds that feels warm and premium rather than sterile. 
- Secondary (30%): Muted Sandstone (e.g., #E6DFD9). Use this soft, warm off-white for your body text and headings to ensure high readability without harsh contrast. 
- Accent (10%): Radiant Terracotta (e.g., #E06D43) or Bright Firozi/Teal (e.g., #00A3A3). Slightly desaturate your accent colors for dark mode to prevent visual vibration, using them for CTA buttons and reservation forms. 
- Card/Surface Backgrounds: Roasted Cacao (e.g., #282320). A slightly lighter dark tone to make interactive food menus, location blocks, and review cards pop against the main background.
